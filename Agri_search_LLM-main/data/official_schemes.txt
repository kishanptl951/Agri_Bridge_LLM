--- PM-KISAN SCHEME RULES (2026) ---
ELIGIBILITY: 
1. All landholding farmer families with cultivable land in their names.
2. The "2-hectare" limit is removed; all land sizes are eligible.
3. Family is defined as: Husband, Wife, and Minor Children.
EXCLUSIONS (Ineligible if any member is):
- A retired/serving govt employee (except Multi-Tasking Staff/Class IV/Group D).
- A monthly pensioner receiving ₹10,000 or more.
- An Income Tax payer in the last assessment year.
- A professional (Doctor, Engineer, Lawyer, CA).

--- STATE IRRIGATION SUBSIDY (PMKSY 2026) ---
ELIGIBILITY:
1. Small Farmers (1 to 2 Hectares) and Marginal Farmers (< 1 Hectare).
BENEFITS:
- 100% Subsidy for Drip/Sprinkler installation for Small/Marginal farmers.
- 75% Subsidy for Other/Big farmers (> 2 Hectares).
- Extra: ₹25,000 subsidy for digging borewells; ₹15,000 for Electric motors.
